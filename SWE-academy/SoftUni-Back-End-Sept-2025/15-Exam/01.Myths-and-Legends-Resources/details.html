Name: Thor

Origin: Norse Mythology

Role: God of Thunder and Storms

Symbol / Attribute: Mjolnir (Hammer), Belt of Strength

Era: Viking Age Legends

Description: Thor is a hammer-wielding god associated with thunder, lightning, storms, oak trees, strength, the protection of mankind, and also hallowing and fertility. He is one of the most important figures in Norse mythology and a central character in many myths and sagas.
